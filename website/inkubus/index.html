Dona

What the demon does

Six gifts, each one a string tied to the work you swore you'd finish.

I

A quota that recomputes

Words remaining ÷ days remaining, recast every dawn. Bank words ahead and tomorrow eases; fall behind and it bares its teeth.

II

The phases of the work

Outline → Act I → Act II → Act III → the final push. Inkubus reads your word count and tells you what the day is actually for.

III

Streak, levels, the long burn

Show up, hit the mark, keep the flame lit. A streak-and-XP rite that rewards the days you returned — and remembers the days you didn't.

IV

The anti-deletion ward

Only surviving words count. Cut into work you'd already committed and you pay in XP and a furious stare; large cuts are stashed, never lost.

V

Compile the manuscript

Bind your included chapters into a clean, standard-format .docx — title page, running header, scene breaks, all of it.

VI

The ever-watching widget

A floating sigil and a menu-bar eye: today's goal ring, your streak, the demon's mood, and a scratch field that still counts.
