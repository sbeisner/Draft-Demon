Meet your deadline. Or meet the demon.

Inkubus is the goal-aligned writing app. Tell it your target — Inkubus turns it into a daily word goal, a phase-by-phase plan, and a streak you'll fight to keep. Delete your hard-won words and it turns... less friendly.

Built to make you finish

Not another blank page. Inkubus is a writing companion with opinions about your goals.

🎯

Daily goal that adapts

Remaining words ÷ remaining days, recomputed every day. Bank words ahead and tomorrow's target drops; fall behind and it climbs.

🗺️

A phase-by-phase plan

Outline → Act 1 → Act 2 → Act 3 → Final push. Inkubus maps your word count to what you should actually be working on today.

🔥

Streaks, XP & levels

A Duolingo-style motivation layer. Show up, hit your goal, keep the streak alive — and watch the demon cheer you on.

🛡️

Anti-deletion guardrails

Only surviving words count. Cut into earlier days' work and you take an XP hit and a stern look. Big cuts get stashed safely, never lost.

📄

Compile to manuscript

Assemble your included chapters into a clean, standard-format .docx — title page, running header, scene breaks, the works.

🪄

Always-on widget

A floating mini-panel and menu-bar nudge: today's goal ring, your streak, Inkubus's current mood, and a quick scratch field.

Two moods. One accountability partner.

Inkubus reacts to how the writing's going. You'll know where you stand at a glance.

😈 Happy Inkubus

You're on track, ahead of pace, or you just hit today's goal. Keep it up — the streak grows.

👿 Angry Inkubus

You've fallen behind, or you're clawing back words you already committed. Time to get back to the page.
